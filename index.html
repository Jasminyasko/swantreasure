The Transformation Game

Playing the Transformation game is an effective way to:

  • clarify important issues
  • resolve conflicts and release negative patterns
  • enhance relationships
  • release fears and open new perspectives
  • find new directions and move forward with ease
  • achieve your goals with grace
  • shine in your radiance/li>
